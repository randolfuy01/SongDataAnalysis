Kanye West

Artist(s): Kanye

Music Type: Rap / Pop

From: Atlanta, Georgia

Popularity index: 95

Kanye West, born in Atlanta in 1977, is a renowned American rapper, producer, and fashion designer. He gained fame for his groundbreaking albums like "The College Dropout" and "My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy," which pushed the boundaries of hip-hop and won critical acclaim. Beyond music, Kanye has ventured into fashion with his Yeezy brand, collaborating with major labels and creating his own line. He is known for his outspoken personality, often making headlines for his candid and controversial statements. Some of his most notable songs includes Flashing Lights (Graduation), Runaway (My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy), and Heartless (808s & Heartbreaks)

Drake

Artist(s): Drake

Music Type: Rap / Pop

From: Toronto, Canda

Popularity index: 99

Drake, born Aubrey Drake Graham in 1986, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, and actor. He first gained recognition through his role on the television series "Degrassi: The Next Generation" and then transitioned into a successful music career. Drake is known for his versatile style, blending hip-hop, R&B, and pop influences, and he has released numerous chart-topping albums like "Views," "Take Care," and "Scorpion." His signature emotional and introspective lyrics have resonated with a global audience, making him one of the most commercially successful and critically acclaimed artists in contemporary music. In addition to his music, Drake is also a savvy entrepreneur and has made significant contributions to the world of fashion and business.
